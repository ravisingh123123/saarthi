About Us

At Mind2Machine, we are building SMART AGRICULTURE – an Intelligent Crop Protection System to help farmers monitor and protect their crops in a smarter, safer, and more cost-effective way. Using AI, IoT sensors, drones, and machine learning, our system detects plant diseases in real-time and applies pesticides only where and when they are truly needed.

AI-Powered Detection

Over 90% accuracy in identifying crop diseases using machine learning models.

Precision Farming

Reduces pesticide usage by up to 70% through targeted application.

Higher Yields

Boosts crop yield by up to 40% while saving farmers nearly $800 per hectare annually.

Easy Monitoring

Farmers can track crop health anytime via mobile or web applications.

Our Vision
